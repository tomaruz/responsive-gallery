A LITTLE MORE ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

As a designer, I enjoy looking at and dissecting other designers' work as it helps me understand the processes and reasoning behind their creative work. Here you will find a list of my favourite websites that I go for when I need some inspiration.

This website also contains a list of links to creative agencies' websites that I discovered, fell in love with and would like to work for in the future! I love going through their websites as they tend to document their design processes well and explain their creative thinking and solutions to tackle each brief.

I have also decided to create a gallery of design work that I found clever and inspiring. The constantly expanding gallery is a selection of my favourite design projects that I love!
